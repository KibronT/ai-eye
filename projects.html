The inaugural Sprintern cohort produced five projects during the Spring 2025 three-week workshop program. Their work covers AI content detection, browser tools, scam awareness, and AI literacy education.

AI vs. Human: Telling the Difference

A linguistic analysis of AI-generated versus human-written news articles. The team used Python to examine emotional tone, sentence complexity, named entities, and transitional language patterns.
