About the Program

What Is a Sprintern?

Sprinterns are undergraduate students at the University of Maryland who take on a hands-on research and design challenge through DetectAIves. The name reflects the sprint-like, fast-paced nature of the work and the real-world experience it provides.

During a three-week workshop program, Sprinterns work in small teams to identify a real challenge related to AI literacy, research it, build something useful, and present their findings publicly.

Sprinterns gain hands-on research, communication, and design skills while contributing work that benefits the broader community.

Real Research

Sprinterns tackle genuine, unresolved questions about AI and its impact on communities.

Real Products

Every team ships something a tool, a guide, a report that goes live on this site.

Real Impact

Their work does not disappear after the program. It stays here, accessible to anyone who needs it.
