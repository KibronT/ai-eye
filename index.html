DetectAIves is an ongoing research and education initiative focused on helping people of all ages understand, question, and navigate artificial intelligence. Through workshops, student projects, and community resources, we make AI accessible to everyone.

What We Do

Learning AI Together

DetectAIves brings together university researchers, students, and community members to build practical AI literacy through workshops, hands on projects, and accessible resources.

AI Literacy Workshops

Hands-on, community centered workshops that teach people how to recognize and use AI tools in everyday life.

Student Research and Projects

Undergraduate and graduate students develop tools, resources, and research that address AI.

Intergenerational Learning

Our programs especially focus on older adults and community members who may deal with rapidly changing technology.
