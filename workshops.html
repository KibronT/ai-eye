DetectAIves Sprintern Program, Summer 2026

June 2026, Three Weeks

The second DetectAIves workshop cohort will take place in June 2026 as a three-week intergenerational program. Undergraduate Sprinterns will work alongside older adults to explore AI literacy challenges, research emerging technologies, and develop practical tools and resources. The program emphasizes collaboration across generations, with teams designing projects that help communities better understand and evaluate AI systems.

Length Three weeks
Format Research, design, and build
Participants Undergraduate students working with older adults through an intergenerational workshop model
Output Five or more projects, presentations, and public AI literacy resources
